=========================================================
🗡️ STARTER ACTIONS 
=========================================================

**Core Starter Actions have no cooldown 
**Advanced Starter Actions gain 1 cooldown per manual Overclock

CORE STARTERS 

1. Quick Slash – 1 AP 
   • ⭐ Base: 6 dmg, +1 Chain
   • 1★ (1 OC): 8 dmg, +2 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 12 dmg, +3 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 16 dmg, +4 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 20 dmg, +6 Chain. If this is your first action this turn, retrigger Quick Slash once (ignores cooldown).

1.5 2x Slash – 1 AP 
   • ⭐ Base: 2x2 dmg, +2 Chain 
   • 1★ (1 OC): 3×2 dmg, +3 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 4×2 dmg, +5 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 6×2 dmg, +7 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 7×2 dmg, +10 Chain 

2. Heavy Slash – 1 AP(fringe adv action?) 
   • ⭐ Base: 8–10 dmg
   • 1★ (1 OC): 14–17 dmg
   • 2★ (2 OC): 22–28 dmg
   • 3★ (3 OC): 35–40 dmg
   • 4★ (4 OC): 55 dmg. If used as the final action of your turn, deal x1.5 dmg.

7. Kunai – 0 AP, 3 Charges per battle (Charge-Based, No Cooldown)
   • ⭐ Base: 7 dmg, +1 Chain
   • 1★ (1 OC): 14 dmg, +2 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 21 dmg, +3 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 28 dmg, +4 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 35 dmg, +5 Chain. This Kunai ignores all Dodge and Shields. 33% chance to recover each Kunai (once per turn).


ADV STARTERS 

3. Hyper Slash – 1 AP
   • ⭐ Base: 2×3 dmg, +2-3 Chain (Hits twice)
   • 1★ (1 OC): 2×4 dmg, +4-5 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 2×7 dmg, +7-9 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 2×12 dmg, +12-15 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 3×14 dmg, +15-18 Chain (Hits three times). If all hits target the same enemy and that enemy dies, gain +15 Chain

4. Arc Blade – 1 AP (AoE)
   • ⭐ Base: 7 dmg (AoE), +1 Chain per enemy hit
   • 1★ (1 OC): 12 dmg (AoE), +2 Chain per enemy hit
   • 2★ (2 OC): 18 dmg (AoE), +3 Chain per enemy hit
   • 3★ (3 OC): 24 dmg (AoE), +4 Chain per enemy hit
   • 4★ (4 OC): 24 dmg (AoE), +4 Chain per enemy hit. Retrigger for each enemy hit. 

5. Rand() Blade – 1 AP (Random Hits)
   • ⭐ Base: 1–3 hits, 3 dmg each, +1 Chain per hit
   • 1★ (1 OC): 2–4 hits, 4 dmg each, +2 Chain per hit
   • 2★ (2 OC): 2–5 hits, 6 dmg each, +2 Chain per hit
   • 3★ (3 OC): 3–6 hits, 7 dmg each, +3 Chain per hit
   • 4★ (4 OC): 4–8 hits, 8 dmg each, +4 Chain per hit. If you roll max hits (8), immediately retrigger Rand() Blade (indefinite).

6. Lacerate (DOT, only one installed instance of Lacerate can be active on a single enemy. If another Lacerate is installed, they can be stacked on a single enemy).
   • ⭐ Base: Inflict 4–6 Bleed per turn for 3 turns (Ignores Dodge/Shield)
   • 1★ (1 OC): Inflict 6–8 Bleed per turn for 3 turns
   • 2★ (2 OC): Inflict 8–10 Bleed per turn for 4 turns
   • 3★ (3 OC): Inflict 12–14 Bleed per turn for 4 turns
   • 4★ (4 OC): Inflict 15–18 Bleed per turn for 4 turns. 

7. Kunai – 0 AP, 3 Charges per battle (Charge-Based, No Cooldown)
   • ⭐ Base: 3 dmg, +1 Chain
   • 1★ (1 OC): 6 dmg, +2 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 9 dmg, +3 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 12 dmg, +4 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 16 dmg, +5 Chain. This Kunai ignores all Dodge and Shields. 33% chance to recover each Kunai (once per turn).


=====================================
💥 FINISHER ACTIONS (v2.9)
=====================================

**Core Finishers have a 1 turn cooldown by default, +1 turn cooldown per manual Overclock 
**Adv Finisher Actions have a 2 turn cooldown by default, +1 turn cooldown per manual Overclock. 

CORE 

1. Rift Cut – 2 AP (Chain Finisher)
   • ⭐ Base: 4 dmg per Chain (spends all Chain)
   • 1★ (1 OC): 5 dmg per Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 7 dmg per Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 10 dmg per Chain, +2 dmg per Shadow Image active
   • 4★ (4 OC): 13 dmg per Chain, +4 dmg per Shadow Image active

ADVANCED 

2. Flurry – 2 AP (Multi-Hit Chain Finisher)
   • ⭐ Base: 3–5 dmg per Chain (spends all Chain)
   • 1★ (1 OC): 5–7 dmg per Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 8–10 dmg per Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 11–12 dmg per Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 14 dmg per Chain. Overkill grants +100% Zenny

3. Slice n' Dice – 3 AP (Spread Chain Finisher)
   • ⭐ Base: 2–6 dmg per Chain (randomly spread across enemies)
   • 1★ (1 OC): 3–7 dmg per Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): 5–8 dmg per Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): 6–9 dmg per Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): 8–12 dmg per Chain. +1 base dmg per unique enemy hit

4. Rupture – 2 AP (Chain-Based Bleed Trigger)
   • ⭐ Base: Inflict 1 Bleed per Chain spent among all enemies. Remove all Chain.
   • 1★ (1 OC): Inflict 1–2 Bleed per Chain on all enemies. Remove all Chain.
   • 2★ (2 OC): Inflict 1–3 Bleed per Chain on all enemies. Remove all Chain.
   • 3★ (3 OC): Inflict 1–4 Bleed per Chain on all enemies. Remove all Chain.
   • 4★ (4 OC): Inflict 2–5 Bleed per Chain on all enemies. Remove all Chain.

🌀 UTILITY ACTIONS (v2.9)
=====================================

**Core Utility Actions have no cooldown 
**Advanced Utility Actions gain 1 cooldown per manual Overclock

CORE UTILITY 

1. Dodge – 1 AP
   • ⭐ Base: +3–5 Dodge
   • 1★ (1 OC): +5–8 Dodge 
   • 2★ (2 OC): +8–11 Dodge
   • 3★ (3 OC): +13–17 Dodge
   • 4★ (4 OC): +Dodge 22–25. Whenever you Perfect Dodge, summon a Shadow Decoy

2. Flicker – 0 AP, 2 Charges per battle (Charge-Based, No Cooldown/Overclock)
   • ⭐ Base: Avoid 5–7


ADV UTILITY 

4. Phase Drift – 1 AP
   • ⭐ Base: +3 Dodge, gain +2 Chain
   • 1★ (1 OC): +5 Dodge, gain +3 Chain
   • 2★ (2 OC): +8 Dodge, gain +4 Chain
   • 3★ (3 OC): +12 Dodge, gain +5 Chain
   • 4★ (4 OC): +15 Dodge, gain 1 Chain for each damage dodged this turn


CORE STARTER UTILITY 

3. Deflect – 1 AP
   • ⭐ Base: +2 Dodge, reflect 50% of damage dodged
   • 1★ (1 OC): +4 Dodge, reflect 100% of damage dodged
   • 2★ (2 OC): +6 Dodge, reflect 200% of damage dodged
   • 3★ (3 OC): +8 Dodge, reflect 400% of damage dodged to all enemies
   • 4★ (4 OC): Deflect all damage twice; bypasses Dodge
